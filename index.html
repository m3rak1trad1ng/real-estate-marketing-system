Both. Independent agents get a reliable structure they can reuse without reinventing marketing every time. Teams and brokerages get one shared standard that keeps output consistent across multiple agents, listings, and contributors.

Yes. Everything is editable in Canva—text, images, colors, logos, and branding elements. The value comes from reusing the underlying structure rather than rebuilding layouts from scratch.
