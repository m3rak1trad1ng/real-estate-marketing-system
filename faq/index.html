FAQ

The Real Estate Marketing System replaces inconsistent marketing materials with a single repeatable standard that stays consistent across listings, campaigns, and agents.

This FAQ explains what the system is, what you receive, Canva requirements, licensing, and delivery so you can purchase with clear expectations.

Is this just a bundle of Canva templates?

No. This is not a collection of one-off designs.

This product is a standardized marketing system: reusable base formats, shared structures, and defined usage rules designed to be applied consistently across listings, campaigns, and team members.

If you want disposable templates that change every time, this is not the right product.

How is this different from buying Canva templates?

Most Canva templates are individual designs meant to be customized once and replaced.

This product is a repeatable system: shared structures, standardized formats, and rules designed to be reused across listings and campaigns without redesigning layouts or re-deciding formatting every time.

What exactly do I receive after purchase?

You receive access to a complete, Canva-based marketing system that is organized by channel and use case.

Instead of isolated templates, you’re getting a structured library of reusable base formats designed to work together across:

  • Social media
  • Print materials
  • Email campaigns
  • Presentations and decks
  • Internal documentation and governance

Every included format is listed on the Full System Table of Contents, which is the authoritative index of what’s included and allows you to validate scope before purchase.

After purchase, the system is duplicated into your Canva workspace so you can keep it, manage it, and use it at your convenience.

Who is this best suited for?

This system is built for real estate professionals who want marketing to operate as an internal standard rather than a recurring design project.

  • Independent agents who want consistent, professional output without reinventing layouts
  • Teams and brokerages that need standardization across multiple agents and listings
  • Marketing coordinators, assistants, and support staff executing materials on behalf of agents
  • Organizations that value repeatability, speed, and control over creative experimentation
Is this built for individual agents or teams?

Both.

Independent agents benefit from having a reliable, professional system in place. Teams and brokerages benefit from shared standards that keep marketing consistent across multiple agents, listings, and contributors.

Do I need a Canva account to use this?

Yes. A Canva account is required.

Canva Free is sufficient to duplicate, edit, and export the included formats. You do not need Canva Pro for the system to function.

Canva Pro is optional and may be useful for convenience features (such as Brand Kits, premium assets, or one-click resizing), but the system is fully usable without Pro.

You are responsible for maintaining your own Canva account (free or paid, at your discretion). This product does not include Canva access or subscriptions.

How do my agents or team members access the system in Canva?

After purchase, the system is duplicated into a Canva workspace controlled by the buyer (typically the brokerage, team lead, or account owner).

From there, there are two common ways teams use it:

Shared workspace approach (recommended for teams)
Management keeps the “master” system in a shared Canva workspace. Agents duplicate the formats they need into their own working folders. This preserves the master system while keeping standards consistent.

Distributed approach
Management duplicates and distributes selected formats to agents or departments as needed.

You do not need everyone using a single Canva login. Access is managed using Canva’s standard sharing and permissions based on how your team is structured.

Exact sharing and permission options may vary depending on your Canva plan.

Do agents need design experience to use this system?

No.

If an agent can edit text and images in Canva, they can use the system effectively without design training. The system is designed to remove layout and formatting decisions during execution.

Can I customize the designs?

Yes. All files are fully editable in Canva.

You can update text, images, colors, logos, and branding elements. The system’s value comes from reusing the underlying structure rather than redesigning layouts from scratch.

Are the margins, layouts, or elements locked?

No.

The system defines standards but does not technically lock files. You can change any element if desired; however, the purpose of the system is to eliminate repeated layout decisions by settling them once.

Does this include photos, listing descriptions, or marketing copy?

No. This system provides structure, not listing content.

You will supply your own property photos, listing details, market data, and written copy specific to your listings or campaigns.

The value is that once those inputs are added, the underlying layout and standards stay consistent across everything you create.

How does this help maintain consistency across a team?

All users work from the same base formats, dimensions, and layout structures.

This ensures marketing remains visually consistent regardless of who creates it, without requiring constant review, redesign, or centralized design bottlenecks.

How long does setup take?

Setup is performed once—typically by management or whoever owns branding.

Time depends on how many brand elements you’re applying, how many people will be using the system, and whether you’re standardizing internal rules across multiple agents or offices. After setup, agents can begin using the system immediately.

Does this replace the need for custom design work or branding?

It replaces routine, repeatable marketing design.

Custom branding or one-off design work may still be appropriate for logos, rebrands, or special campaigns outside the system.

Can this be used across multiple agents or offices?

The license allows use within a single organization.

“Single organization” means one business entity (and its internal team members) using the system for its own listings and marketing. Internal use across multiple agents, departments, and locations under that same organization is permitted.

The system may not be shared, resold, or redistributed to other brokerages, separate business entities, or outside organizations. If your structure is complex (multiple entities, franchises, subsidiaries), use the Purchase page to ask before buying.

Can I share this with assistants, VAs, or contractors?

Yes—if they are working within your organization and creating materials on your behalf.

“VA” means virtual assistant. Contractors are allowed if they are executing marketing for your organization (not reusing the system for other clients).

The system may not be shared with third parties for reuse across other clients or organizations.

How is access delivered after purchase?

Access is delivered immediately after checkout.

You receive organized Canva files that can be duplicated into your Canva workspace and used without delay. Once duplicated, the system lives in your Canva environment so your team can access it at your convenience.

Is this a one-time purchase or a subscription?

This is a one-time purchase.

There are no subscriptions, renewals, or recurring fees for the product itself.

Are there any additional costs?

No. The product itself is a one-time purchase.

Canva Pro is optional. Some buyers choose it for convenience features (like Brand Kits, premium assets, or resizing tools), but it is not required to use the system.

Do you provide training or onboarding?

This is a self-serve product.

The system includes clear structure and internal documentation designed to make onboarding straightforward without live training or ongoing support.

Are refunds available?

Because this is a digital system delivered immediately, all sales are final.
