FAQ

This FAQ is built to remove confusion. It explains what the system is, what you receive at purchase, Canva requirements, licensing, delivery, and refunds—so you can make an informed buying decision with clear expectations.

The Real Estate Marketing System is a Canva-based marketing system designed to bring structure and consistency to real estate marketing by replacing fragmented, one-off materials with a single, repeatable standard used across listings, campaigns, and agents.

Is this just a bundle of Canva templates?

No. This is not a collection of one-off designs.

This product is a standardized marketing system: reusable base formats, shared structures, and defined usage rules designed to be applied consistently across listings, campaigns, and team members without redesigning layouts each time.

If you are looking for individual, disposable templates, this is not the right product.

How is this different from buying Canva templates?

Most Canva templates are individual designs meant to be customized once and replaced.

This product is a standardized marketing system: shared structures, base formats, and usage rules designed to be reused across listings, campaigns, and teams. Design decisions are made once and applied consistently instead of being revisited for every new piece of marketing.

What exactly do I receive after purchase?

You receive access to a complete, Canva-based marketing system that is organized by channel and use case.

Instead of isolated templates, you’re getting a structured library of reusable base formats designed to work together across:

  • Social media
  • Print materials
  • Email campaigns
  • Presentations and decks
  • Internal documentation and governance

Every included format is listed on the Full System Table of Contents, which is the authoritative index of what’s included. This allows you to validate scope before purchase.

After purchase, the system is delivered in an organized format that can be duplicated into your Canva account and used immediately.

Who is this best suited for?

This system is built for real estate professionals who want marketing to operate as an internal system rather than a recurring design project.

  • Brokerages and team leaders managing multiple agents
  • Agents who want consistent, professional output without redesigning each time
  • Organizations that value repeatability, speed, and control over creative experimentation
Is this built for individual agents or teams?

Both.

Independent agents benefit from having a reliable, professional system in place, while teams and brokerages benefit from shared standards that keep marketing aligned across multiple agents, listings, and contributors.

Can this be used across multiple agents or offices?

The license allows use within a single organization.

You may use the system across multiple agents, listings, and internal team members under the same organization. It may not be shared, resold, or redistributed outside that organization.

Do I need a Canva account to use this?

Yes. A Canva account is required.

Canva Free is sufficient to duplicate, edit, and export the included formats. You do not need Canva Pro for the system to function.

Canva Pro may be helpful for optional features (such as Brand Kits, background removal, or premium stock assets), but those features are not required to use this system as intended.

You are responsible for maintaining your own Canva account (free or paid, at your discretion). This product does not include Canva access or subscriptions.

Does Canva Free work, or do I need Canva Pro?

Canva Free works.

Canva Pro is optional. Many teams choose Pro for convenience features like Brand Kits, premium assets, and background removal, but the system is fully usable without Pro.

Do agents need design experience to use this system?

No.

If an agent can edit text and images in Canva, they can use the system effectively without design training. The system is designed to remove the need for layout or formatting decisions during execution.

Can I customize the designs?

Yes. All files are fully editable in Canva.

You can update text, images, colors, logos, and branding elements. The system’s value comes from reusing the underlying structure rather than redesigning layouts from scratch.

Are the margins, layouts, or elements locked?

No.

The system defines standards but does not technically lock files. You can change any element if desired; however, the purpose of the system is to eliminate repeated layout decisions by settling them once.

Does this include photos, listing descriptions, or marketing copy?

No. This system provides structure, not listing content.

You will supply your own property photos, listing details, market data, and written copy specific to your listings or campaigns.

The value of the system is that once those inputs are added, the underlying layout and structure stay consistent across everything you create.

How does this help maintain consistency across a team?

All users work from the same base formats, dimensions, and layout structures.

This ensures marketing remains visually consistent regardless of who creates it, without requiring constant review, redesign, or centralized design bottlenecks.

How long does setup take?

Setup is performed once by management.

This typically involves duplicating the system into a Canva account, applying brand elements, and defining internal usage guidelines. After setup, agents can begin using the system immediately.

Does this replace the need for custom design work or branding?

It replaces routine, repeatable marketing design.

Custom branding or one-off design work may still be appropriate for logos, rebrands, or special campaigns outside the system.

Can I share this with assistants, VAs, or contractors?

Yes, if they are working within your organization and creating materials on your behalf.

The system may not be shared with third parties for reuse across other clients or organizations.

How is access delivered after purchase?

Access is delivered immediately after checkout.

You receive organized Canva files that can be duplicated and used without delay.

Is this a one-time purchase or a subscription?

This is a one-time purchase.

There are no subscriptions, renewals, or recurring fees.

Are there any additional costs?

No. The product itself is a one-time purchase.

Canva Pro is optional. Some buyers choose it for convenience features (Brand Kit, premium assets, background remover), but it is not required to use the system.

Do you provide training or onboarding?

This is a self-serve product.

The system includes clear structure and internal documentation designed to make onboarding straightforward without live training or ongoing support.

Are refunds available?

Because this is a digital system delivered immediately, all sales are final.
