How Access Works

This is a one-time purchase. Access is provided immediately after checkout.

After purchase, you receive access to the complete set of organized Canva files. You can duplicate the system into your Canva account and begin applying your branding and usage rules.

  • Delivery: Organized Canva files (ready to duplicate and use)
  • Access timing: Immediately after purchase
  • Updates: None required; the system is delivered as-is
If you experience access or delivery issues, check your purchase confirmation first. If it still isn’t resolved, contact support using the method provided at checkout.

What You Receive

You receive a complete, standardized marketing system designed for repeatable execution across listings and campaigns. All templates are editable in Canva and intended to be duplicated and reused.

  • Standardized base formats across key marketing channels
  • Defined structure and consistent sizing for repeat execution
  • Files designed to keep teams aligned without redesigning each time

Next Steps After Purchase

Setup is performed once by management. Typically this includes duplicating the system, applying brand elements, and defining internal usage guidelines. After setup, agents can begin using the system immediately.
