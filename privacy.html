Privacy Policy

This policy explains the limited information collected through this website, which is restricted to what is necessary to process purchases and deliver the product.

Overview

Operational Systems, LLC operates this website to provide information about The Real Estate Marketing System and to support purchase and delivery.

We do not sell personal information and do not collect personal data beyond what is required to complete a transaction and deliver access to the product.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information (such as name and email address) if you contact us or request support.
  • Transaction-related information associated with a purchase (such as confirmation details or delivery status). Payment details are handled by third-party processors.
  • Communications you send to us (such as emails or form submissions).

We do not collect behavioral profiles, marketing data, or unnecessary personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Advertising or tracking data
  • Marketing analytics or profiling data
  • Sensitive personal information
  • Precise location data beyond standard web request information

How We Use Information

  • Process and confirm purchases
  • Deliver access to the product
  • Respond to support requests or communications
  • Maintain basic website functionality

We do not use personal information for advertising, marketing, or unsolicited outreach.

Third-Party Services

This website relies on limited third-party services strictly to process payments and deliver access to the product.

We do not use third-party services for advertising, tracking, or marketing analytics.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as needed to complete transactions, provide access to purchased materials, and maintain required business records.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect the limited information we handle.

Your Rights

If you have questions about information associated with a purchase or communication, you may contact us for clarification.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page.
