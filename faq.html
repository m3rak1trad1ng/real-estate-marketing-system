Product & Scope

How is this different from buying Canva templates?
Most Canva templates are individual designs meant to be customized once and replaced. This product is a standardized marketing system: shared structures, base formats, and usage rules designed to be reused across listings, campaigns, and teams without redesigning layouts each time.
What exactly do I receive when I purchase?
You receive access to a complete system of 117 standardized base formats spanning social media, email, print, presentations, and internal documentation. All formats are organized, governed, and designed to support consistent, repeatable marketing outputs over time.
Is this built for individual agents or teams?
Both. Independent agents benefit from having a reliable, professional system in place, while teams and brokerages benefit from shared standards that keep marketing aligned across multiple agents, listings, and contributors.
Does this replace the need for custom design work or branding?
The system replaces routine, repeatable marketing design. Custom branding or design work may still be appropriate for logos, brand development, or special campaigns outside the system.

Setup & Usage

Can I customize the designs?
Yes. All files are fully editable in Canva. Text, images, colors, and branding elements can be updated. The system’s value comes from reusing the underlying structure rather than redesigning layouts from scratch.
Are the margins, layouts, or elements locked?
No. The system defines standards but does not technically lock files. Users may change any element if desired; however, the purpose of the system is to make layout decisions once rather than revisiting them repeatedly.
Do agents need design experience to use this system?
No. The system is designed to be usable by non-designers. If an agent can edit text and images in Canva, they can use the system effectively without design training.
How does this help maintain consistency across a team?
All agents and contributors work from the same base formats, dimensions, and layout structures. This ensures marketing remains visually consistent regardless of who creates the materials, without requiring constant review or oversight.
How long does setup take?
Setup is performed once by management. This typically involves duplicating the system into a Canva account, applying brand elements, and defining internal usage guidelines. After setup, agents can begin using the system immediately.

Purchase, Delivery & Licensing

How is access delivered after purchase?
Access is provided immediately after purchase. Buyers receive organized Canva files that can be duplicated and used without delay.
Is this a one-time purchase or a subscription?
This is a one-time purchase. There are no subscriptions, renewals, or recurring fees.
Are there any usage limits or licensing restrictions?
The system is licensed for internal use within a single organization. It may be used across listings, campaigns, and team members. Internal sharing with agents or staff within the organization is permitted. The system may not be resold, redistributed, or shared externally.
Are refunds available?
Because this is a digital system delivered immediately, all sales are final. Buyers are encouraged to review the full buyer-facing table of contents prior to purchase to confirm suitability.
