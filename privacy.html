Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect, how it’s used, and the choices available to you.

Overview

Operational Systems, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates this website to provide information about The Real Estate Marketing System and to support access and delivery for purchasers.

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website and related communications.

This website may use third-party services for payments, email communication, analytics, or file delivery. Those providers may collect information under their own policies.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address) when you request access, make a purchase, or contact us.
  • Transaction information related to purchases (e.g., purchase confirmation, product access status). Payment details are typically processed by third-party payment providers.
  • Usage data (e.g., pages viewed, approximate device/browser info) if analytics are enabled on the site.
  • Communications you send to us (e.g., emails or form messages).

How We Use Information

We use information for purposes such as:

  • Providing access and delivering the product
  • Responding to inquiries and support requests
  • Processing transactions and confirming purchases (via third-party providers)
  • Maintaining site security and preventing abuse

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate the website and deliver the product (for example: payment processing, email delivery, analytics, or file delivery).

We may also disclose information if required to comply with applicable law or to protect our rights and the security of the site.

Cookies & Analytics

If enabled, cookies or similar technologies may be used to support basic site functionality and understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information collected through the website. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes such as providing access, maintaining records, resolving disputes, and complying with legal obligations.
