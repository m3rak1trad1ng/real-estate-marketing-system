Privacy Policy

This policy explains the limited information we collect through this website, which is restricted to what’s necessary to process purchases and deliver the product.

Overview

Operational Systems, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates this website to provide information about The Real Estate Marketing System and to support purchase and delivery.

This site is not designed to collect personal data beyond what is required to complete a transaction and deliver access to the product.

Information We Collect

We collect only limited information necessary to operate the website, process purchases, and deliver the product.

This may include:

  • Contact information (such as name and email address) if you contact us directly or request support.
  • Transaction-related information associated with a purchase (such as confirmation details or delivery status). Payment information is processed by third-party payment providers and is not stored by us.
  • Communications you send to us, including emails or other direct correspondence.

We do not collect behavioral profiles, marketing data, or unnecessary personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Advertising or tracking data
  • Marketing analytics or user profiling data
  • Sensitive personal information
  • Location data beyond what is inherently provided by standard web requests

If analytics are enabled at any point, they are used only in aggregate to understand general site usage—not individual behavior.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Process and confirm purchases
  • Deliver access to the product
  • Respond to inquiries or support requests
  • Maintain basic website functionality

We do not use personal information for advertising, remarketing, or unsolicited outreach.

Third-Party Services

This website relies on limited third-party services strictly to process payments and deliver access to purchased products. These providers may process information necessary to complete those actions under their own privacy policies.

We do not use third-party services for advertising, tracking, or marketing analytics.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to complete transactions, provide access to purchased materials, and maintain required business records. We do not retain personal information longer than necessary for these purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect the limited information we handle. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Rights

If you have questions about the information associated with a purchase or communication, you may contact us for clarification. Because we collect minimal data, there are limited records subject to access or modification.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational or legal changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how information is handled, contact details are provided on the website.
