The Problem It Solves

Most real estate marketing fails not because of effort—but because there is no system.

Where it breaks down

  • Every new listing or campaign starts from a different place
  • The same core pieces are rebuilt over and over
  • Marketing looks acceptable in isolation, but inconsistent across channels
  • Design decisions are revisited repeatedly instead of settled once
  • Marketing exists, but never becomes operationally repeatable
  • Multiple agents using different tools, layouts, and processes—resulting in inconsistent marketing and constant oversight

This creates wasted time, inconsistent branding, and marketing that can’t scale beyond the person creating it.

FAQ

How is this different from buying Canva templates?
Most Canva templates are individual designs meant to be customized once and replaced. This is a standardized marketing system—shared structures, formats, and rules designed to be reused across listings, campaigns, and teams without redesigning each time.
What exactly do I receive when I purchase?
You receive access to a complete system of 130+ standardized base formats across social media, email, and print—organized, governed, and built to support repeatable marketing over time.
Is this built for individual agents or teams?
Both. Independent agents benefit from consistent, professional output, while teams and brokerages gain a shared structure that keeps marketing aligned across multiple agents, listings, and channels.
Can I customize the designs?
Yes. All files are fully editable in Canva. You can update text, images, colors, and branding while the underlying structure maintains consistency.
Are the margins and layouts locked?
No. The standards are defined, not technically locked. You can change them—but the value comes from having decisions made once instead of revisiting them for every campaign.
Do agents need design experience to use this?
No. If an agent can edit text and images in Canva, they can use the system without design expertise or layout decision-making.
How does this help keep a team consistent?
Everyone works from the same base formats, dimensions, and structure. This ensures marketing looks unified regardless of who creates it—without constant oversight or redesign.
How long does it take to get set up?
Initial setup is handled once by management. This typically involves duplicating the system, applying branding, and defining internal usage. After that, agents can use the system immediately.
Does this replace the need for custom design work?
It replaces routine, repeatable marketing design. Custom design may still be used for special projects or branding work outside the system.
How is access delivered after purchase?
Access is delivered automatically after checkout. You receive organized Canva files that can be duplicated and used immediately.
Is this a one-time purchase or a subscription?
This is a one-time purchase. There are no subscriptions or recurring fees.
Are there any usage limits or restrictions?
The system is licensed for internal use within your organization only. It may be used across your listings, campaigns, and team, but may not be resold, shared, or distributed outside your organization.
Are refunds available?
Because this is a digital system delivered immediately, all sales are final. Please review the full system table of contents before purchasing to ensure it’s the right fit.
